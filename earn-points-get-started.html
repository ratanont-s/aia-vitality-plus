How to start your AIA Vitality journey

Create Account

Download AIA Vitality application and create an account

The more points you earn, the higher your AIA Vitality status, and the more rewards you can enjoy.

Earn points by completing online assessments, exercising, getting health check and completing challenges.

When you become an AlA Vitality member, you will start with 0 Vitality Point with Bronze status. When you choose to live a healthy life by having a regular exercise, eating healthy food or taking an annual health check, you will earn Vitality Points.

AIA Viatily Point Sutructure

Points run for one membership year and start at O at the beginning of the new membership year. Statuses are carried over to the next year and can be increased with new points earned in the new membership year.
